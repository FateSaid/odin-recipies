Hamburger

Hamburger is one of the popular food that is enjoyed all around the world. The best burger recipe for summertime grilling! This juicy burger is jam-packed with all kinds of stuff and has no tasteless bread crumbs. Serve on buns with your favorite condiments.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1.5 onion, finely chopped
  • 1.5 cup shreadded Colby jack or Chedder cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1(ounce) envelope dry onion soup mix
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp dried parsley
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1.5 tsp crushed dried rosemary
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and ligtly oil the grate.
  2. Meanwhile, combine ground beef, onion, cheese, egg, onion soup mix, minced garlic, garlic powder, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, parsley, basil, oregano, rosemary, salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Use your hands to form the mixture into 4 patties.
  3. Cook patties on the preheated grill until no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, about 4 to 5 minutes per side. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
