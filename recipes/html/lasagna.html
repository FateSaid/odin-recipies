Lasagna

Making a lasagna can be time consuming but following these easy-to-learn steps will help you make a quick and delicious lasagna. Don't be stressed if you don't have all of the ingredients listed below. You can even swap the ingredients if you're lactose intolerant or a vegan. There's multiple different ways of making a lasanga so no need to worry about which is the right recipe. Everyone has different tastebuds so it's impossible to please everyone.

These are the ingredients

  • Grounded beef
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato product
  • sugar
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Cheese
  • Egg

These are the steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna acoording to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
